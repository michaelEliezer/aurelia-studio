Philosophy

Designed for Rooms
That Breathe

We believe in restraint. Not emptiness — intention. Every object we create should earn its place in a room by what it adds to the light, the silence, the texture of a space.

Our materials are chosen for how they age. Brass that develops a warmer patina. Stone that holds the memory of touch. Ceramic that reveals its handmade origin. These are not products. They are quiet additions to how you live.

Materials

Tactile by Nature

We work with materials that respond to time, touch, and light. Each piece carries the character of its origin.

Smoked Oak

Darkened through natural smoke treatment, creating deep grain character

Travertine Stone

Quarried and hand-finished, each piece carries unique mineral patterns

Brushed Brass

Living metal that develops a warm patina with handling and time

Raw Ceramic

Unglazed stoneware fired at high temperature, revealing natural texture

Linen Paper

Handmade paper pressed from long-fiber linen, developed for the studio

Amber Glass

Blown glass with mineral tint, developed exclusively for scent vessels

Process

From Study
to Edition

Research

Every collection begins with material investigation — visiting quarries, workshops, and archives to understand what a material wants to become.

Material Study

We work directly with artisans to develop finishes, forms, and processes that honor the material's natural character.

Prototype

Each piece goes through 15 to 40 prototype iterations before final approval. Form, weight, and balance are tested by hand.

Final Edition

Limited editions of 6 to 48 pieces, each numbered and accompanied by a material origin card.
